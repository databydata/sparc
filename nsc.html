Case Study VGA

Volkswagen Group Australia (VGA) is a VW National Sales Company. Operations of VGA includes Import, Sales & Service of VW Passenger Vehicles, VW Commercial Vehicles, Skoda, Audi, CUPRA vehicles and Aftersales business for these brands. AIMS (Australian Import Management System ) is a strategic transformational project to replace legacy system IDS by SAP S/4 HANA in Q1 2025.

Location

Anhui, China

Started

2020

Project Type

Greenfield

Objectives

  • Implement end to end solution by replacing AS-400 system with SAP S4HANA system along with group and local interfaces to be re-integrated.
  • It proposes to ease operations, improve functionalities delivered and robustness of the system, enhance visibility and transparency, drive automation and digitization and support better forecasting based on SAP S/4HANA , Salesforce and MuleSoft.

Achievements

  • Process optimization which helped in scalability of solution. E.g. cross docking , picking and put away.
  • Implemented SAP standard credit management solution (FSCM) for Vehicle Wholesale and Fleet Business.
  • Business alignment on standardization and automation on business process harmonization. Easy to replicate for any new Brands (Cupra).
