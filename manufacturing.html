Manufacturing Plant Story

Volkswagen Group's first dedicated NEV joint venture in China.

Case Study Anhui

Volkswagen Anhui established in 2017, is the Group’s first dedicated NEV joint venture in China. It focuses on the R&D and manufacturing of NEVs and it is VW Group’s third MEB plant in China. VWA will produce EV based on the Group's MEB platform and the first vehicle is expected to roll off the production line in December 2023.

Location

Anhui, China

Started

2020

Project Type

Greenfield

Objectives

  • VWITS partnered with VWA to implement SAP S/4HANA which integrates traditional and new age solutions across diverse operations like Finance, Production, Warehouse, Procurement, Sales etc. including all the local & global interfaces.
  • Ensuring the project progression seamlessly & guiding through its intricate aspects, including business operations and go-to-market plans in this greenfield implementation.

Achievements

  • Enhanced Procurement processes implemented since Nov 2021 have been successfully integrated into business operations, enabling further system optimization.
  • Robust Financial systems are more system driven through streamlined processes & more aligned with Group regulatory & statutory compliance.
  • Designed & implemented, the cutting-edge warehousing solution (EWM) for the futuristic business of the time i.e. EV manufacturing. Being “first” of a kind & will also act as a template for other warehouse solutions are to be upgraded within group.
  • Global interfaces complement CKD Imports, Local Part purchase, Machine Execution, Material Master creation are rolled out & integrated with S4 HANA which are getting slowly matured with every day put into use by the business.
