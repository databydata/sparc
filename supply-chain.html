Supply Chain Collaboration

Enables you to collaborate on and automate procurement transactions on a single networked platform, improving inventory visibility, quality control, and management.

Capability SSG

Supply chain collaboration refers to how buyers and their trading partners can exchange information digitally to ensure supply chain resiliency across forecasts, orders, inventory, logistics, and quality. Dashboards increase visibility into supply chain issues and provide alerts for rapid resolution.

Supply chain visibility enables risk mitigation and impact avoidance and management by providing transparency and insights that make supply chains more agile, optimized, sustainable, and resilient to disruption.
